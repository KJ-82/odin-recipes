Lasagna

A Flavory, Quick Meal For A Large Family

This simple meal is comprised of all macnutrients.

This includes carbs, proteins, and fats.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato
  • Parmesan
  • Onion
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta per package instructions.
  2. Cook meat until color is uniform and not red. Add pasta sauce and cook for 5 mins. Stir cheese into the bowl.
  3. Layer the pasta wiht the combined ingredients from above
  4. Bake in oven until golden, brown.
