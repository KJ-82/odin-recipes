Pizza

Another Quick Meal For A Large Family

This simple meal is also comprised of all macnutrients.

This includes carbs, proteins, and fats.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato
  • Pepperoni
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Knead the dough. Roll the dough out to make a circle.
  2. Pour a conservative amount of tomato sauce.
  3. Sprinkle cheese to create a uniform layer.
  4. Place pepperonis on pizza.
  5. Bake in the oven at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes
