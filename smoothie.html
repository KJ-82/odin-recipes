Smoothie

A Quick Meal For The On the Go Parent

This simple meal is amazingly comprised of all macnutrients.

This includes carbs, proteins, and fats.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Protein Powder
  • Banana
  • Peanut Butter
  • Avacodo

Steps

  1. Pour 18oz into a blender.
  2. Put in two scopes of protein powder.
  3. Put in a whole banana.
  4. Insert two tablespoons of peanut butter.
  5. Add half or a whole avacodo.
